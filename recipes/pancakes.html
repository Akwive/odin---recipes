Pancakes - a common dish that you can never go wrong with.

Obviously, yours won't look like this! But it's the taste that matters.

==============================================================================================================================

==============================================================================================================================

~Ingredients~

  • 400g (1 1/2 cups) of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (3 if you're like me)
  • A pinch of salt, or more according to taste
  • 200ml (1 1/4) of milk
  • 1 egg (yes! you can make much more than you think with only one.)
  • baking oil or butter (whichever you prefer)
  • berries or jam

~Preparation steps~

  1. Mix the eggs well (the better you mix the better the pancakes will be!), add a bit of salt and a tablespoon of warm water.
  2. Add the milk, mix again.
  3. Add the sugar, then the flour - sift it!!!
  4. prepare the pan, oil (or butter), wait for it to reach medium heat.
  5. take a spoon and pour the batter on the pan, forming round shapes (it's okay if they connect, wait for them to bake a bit and separate them with a spatula)
  6. Wait until both sides are nicely brown, and take them out
  7. Serve with your favourite berries or jam and enjoy!

Tip: if you want to drain the pancakes from oil, place them on a paper napkin on a plate! This will absorb what's unwanted.
